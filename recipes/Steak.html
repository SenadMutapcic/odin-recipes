Steak

Steak is a classic and delicious dish loved by many.

Indulge in the savory flavors of a perfectly cooked steak with our special Steak Recipe. Follow the steps below to create a mouthwatering steak that will impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 4 beef steaks (ribeye or sirloin)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps to make delicious Steak

  1. Preheat the grill or a grill pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Brush steaks with olive oil and season with minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and black pepper.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and honey to create a marinade.
  4. Marinate the steaks in the mixture for at least 30 minutes, ensuring they are well coated.
  5. Grill the steaks to your desired level of doneness, typically 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  6. Remove from the grill and let the steaks rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
